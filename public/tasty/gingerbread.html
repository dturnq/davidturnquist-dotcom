Step 1: Mix dry ingredients

1 cups whole wheat flour

1/4 cups almond flour

1 teaspoon baking soda

1/2 teaspoon salt

2 teaspoons ground ginger, heaping

1 teaspoon ground cinnamon, heaping

1/4 teaspoon ground cloves, heaping

 

Step 2: Mix wet ingredients

4 tablespoons salted butter, melted

2/3 cup packed dark brown sugar, but try just 1/3 because it's a bit sweet

2/3 cup molasses

3/4 cup boiling milk

1 large egg

 

Step 3: Pour dry into wet

Mix until evenly smooth

 

Step 4: Oven

butter + flour 9x9 pan

350deg

30-35 mins
